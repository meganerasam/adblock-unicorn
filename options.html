Welcome to Ad Block Unicorn!

Enhance your browsing experience by blocking unwanted ads and protecting yourself from phishing threats. Our extension ensures a cleaner, safer, and more efficient online journey.

Ad Block Unicorn provides you with powerful tools to control your online environment, giving you peace of mind and a more enjoyable browsing experience.

How It Works

Ad Block Unicorn operates by intercepting network requests to known ad and phishing domains, preventing unwanted content from loading and alerting you to potential threats.

Our extension continuously updates its databases to ensure you are protected against the latest advertising techniques and phishing schemes.
